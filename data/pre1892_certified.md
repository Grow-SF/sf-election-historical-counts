# Pre-1892 certified SF-County figures (denominators) — gathered 2026-06-17

Certified San Francisco **County** totals (sum of all candidates in the top
statewide contest) for the 19th-century generals we are extending the night-count
record back to. Denominators only; night counts captured separately from the SF
Chronicle (NewsBank). Sources: CA Statement of Vote (archive.org
`statementofvoteo1886cali` holds **1882 + 1886** read directly), California Blue
Book / State Roster (HathiTrust), and Wikipedia by-county tables (which cite the
CA State Archives "Abstract of the Vote"). Two agents, cross-checked.

## Notes
- NewsBank coverage probe (2026-06-17) confirms SF newspaper issues exist for
  every one of these dates back to **1867** (the earlier "no Chronicle before
  1892" was a capture artifact of the broken-session period, NOT real absence).
  Headlines seen on the entry-search: 1880 "Tidal Wave. Latest Returns ...
  Garfield"; 1886 "Some Phases of the Election"; 1882 explicit "San Francisco
  Chronicle: Page 3"; 1867 "Result of the Election".
- Night counts to be captured from the day-after (and 2-day) issue, SF "Vote of
  the City" / returns table. Hand-count era → expect partial night shares.
