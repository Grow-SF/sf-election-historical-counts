# Election-night share — source-by-source verification sheet

> **Purpose.** Every election-night number in the county comparison, with its **numerator**
> (ballots counted in the final election-night report), its **denominator** (certified-final
> ballots), the computed **share**, and the **exact source URL** + what to look for on that
> page, so each figure can be opened and checked by hand. Generated from
> `data/research/election-night-v3/*.json`.

**Metric:** election-night % = ballots in the *last* report posted on election night ÷ certified-final ballots.
It is **not** the 8 p.m. first tranche (undercounts the night ~2×) and **not** a days-later canvass update.

**Denominator for every CA county** is the same authoritative document: the California Secretary of State
*Statement of Vote → "Voter Participation Statistics by County"* PDF for that election (the county's
**"Total Voters" / Ballots Cast** line). Those URLs are listed once per row below.

**Confidence:** `primary` = official registrar release / archived live results page · 
`secondary` = local news or a derived/proxy figure · `none` = numerator not sourceable (left blank).
