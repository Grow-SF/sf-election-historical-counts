## Method note: separating SD County precincts from other-county wire dispatches

Section 2.11 ("YESTERDAY'S BATTLE") is laid out as two adjacent physical
newspaper columns of the SAME article: column 3 (city wards, First–Fifth, each
headed by a plain bold-caps ward name) and column 4 (county precincts: El
Cajon, Bernardo, Green Valley, Oceanside, Poway, Perris, National City,
Temecula, Lyons — plain bold-caps place names, NO dateline). By contrast,
section 2.12's wire items are headed `PLACENAME, Nov. 2.—` (comma + date +
em-dash) for OTHER counties (Hollister/San Benito, Fresno, San Mateo, San Jose/
Santa Clara, San Buenaventura/Ventura, Colton/Riverside/San Bernardino, etc.).
This formatting distinction (no-date bold header = SD County precinct in 2.11;
dated wire dispatch = other-county in 2.12) is how city/county precincts were
separated from the statewide noise. San Diego County in 1886 still included the
Perris/Temecula/Elsinore area (Riverside County was not split off until 1893),
so Perris and Temecula are correctly SD County precincts, not Riverside County.

## Precincts-reported basis

14 named precincts (5 city wards + 9 county precincts: El Cajon, Bernardo,
Green Valley, Oceanside, Poway, Perris, National City, Temecula, Lyons) printed
in the day-after issue. The article gives no county-wide precinct COUNT (unlike
1908/1920's "X of Y precincts" framing), so the denominator of a precincts-
ratio can't be constructed; only the raw floor vs. the certified contest total
is checkable. Given 1886 San Diego County's actual geographic size (it then
included the modern Riverside County backcountry down to the Colorado River),
many outlying precincts (Julian, Campo, Jamul, Ballena, Encinitas, San Luis
Rey, etc.) are absent from this issue — consistent with a partial night count.

## Verdict

**Night count FLOOR = 1,031 ballots** in the Governor contest, covering 5 San
Diego city wards + 9 named county precincts printed in the Union's Wednesday-
morning (day-after) edition. Per-candidate column totals: Swift (R) 583,
Bartlett (D) 354, Russell (Proh) 73, Wigginton (Am) 10, O'Donnell (Ind) 11;
583+354+73+10+11 = 1,031, matching the row-sum grand total exactly
(cross-check PASS). Share of certified contest total: 1,031 / 2,826 = **36.5%**
(denominator from `denominators.md`, SOV 1886 p.11).
