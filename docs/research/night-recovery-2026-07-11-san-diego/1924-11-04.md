## Cross-check: table's own Totals row + prose

- Totals row (bottom of table): 7,465 precincts in state, 3,763 reported,
  Coolidge 376,338, La Follette 193,830, Davis 51,555, Feris 5,907.
- Adjacent prose confirms an EARLIER (11 o'clock) AP snapshot for context:
  "At 11 o'clock the Associated Press in San Francisco had tabulated 2,323
  complete and incomplete precincts of the 7,465 in the state, with the
  following result: Coolidge 216,713; La Follette 115,788; Davis 30,996, and
  Faris 4,047. Coolidge was leading in ... San Diego ..." — this earlier
  snapshot's statewide sub-totals are all lower than the table's own Totals
  row (216,713 < 376,338 etc.), confirming the printed "State Election Table"
  is a LATER, more complete compilation than the 11 o'clock AP flash quoted
  in the prose — internally consistent.

## Verdict

**Night count FLOOR = 23,961 ballots** in the presidential contest (Coolidge
12,350 + La Follette 9,737 + Davis 1,661 + Feris 213), 158 of 274 San Diego
County precincts, per the Sacramento Daily Union's printed "State Election

remote-paper floor, not San Diego's own compilation; likely understates the
true election-night San Diego Union count had it survived to this era. Share
of certified contest total: 23,961 / 46,408 = **51.6%** (denominator: SOV
1924 President contest sum, `denominators.md`).
