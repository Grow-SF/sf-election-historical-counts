## Arithmetic

Governor contest sum (all candidates as printed) = 2,726 + 1,776 = **4,502 ballots**
counted in the governor contest, 63 of 221 precincts ("complete returns" from those
63). If the smudged digit is a 3 (Stephens 2,736), the sum is 4,512.

## Precincts-reported basis

- 63 of 221 county precincts (ratio 0.285 ≤ 1, gate PASS). The article separately
  says the county has "221 precincts... of which 186 are in the city and 35 in the
  outlying districts" (the 186/35 split is OCR-garbled — OCR printed "661"/"69" —
  but the returns line "63 precincts out of 221" is image-verified).
- Timing: figures compiled by press time of the Wednesday morning edition; the AP
  lead is clocked "1:30 o'clock this [morning]". The count was slow: long ballot
  (~55 amendments), rain, wartime, influenza epidemic; "election officials... were
  still busily engaged in the count early this morning."
- Turnout context printed: registration total ~41,661 (city ~29.5k + county — digits
  OCR-garbled); "estimated last night that not more than 60 percent of this vote had
  been cast."

## Verification pass 2026-07-11 (adversarial re-read)

Tight crops (6-9x LANCZOS upscale via `crop_helper.py`-style crop) of the existing
captures `sd_19181106_p1_crop_gov3.png` and the alternate full-color capture
`sd_19181106_p1_zda.png` (6184x4552 native — same article rendered without the green
highlight overlay used in some other crops, giving a cleaner read).

1. **Ask #1 (Stephens digit, `verify_1918_stephens_digits3.png` /
   `verify_1918_stephens_black.png`, two independent crops from two different source

   2736.
2. **Ask #2 (precinct basis, `sd_19181106_p1_crop_pcts.png` context)** — "complete returns

3. **Ask #3 (DA line, `verify_1918_utley_black3.png`, 9x upscale)** — "Utley 2[?]41,
