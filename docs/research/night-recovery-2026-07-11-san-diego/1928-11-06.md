## Numerator: two successive San Diego County wire flashes, SAME page/edition

Verbatim, vision-read from `probe_sdu1928_sandiego.png`:

> "SAN DIEGO COUNTY — 17 out of 310 precincts, Republican 3,063, Democratic
> 1,452, Socialist 17, Prohibition 19."
>
> "SAN DIEGO COUNTY — 31 out of 310 precincts, Republican 3,533, Democratic
> 1,799, socialist 45, Prohibition 55; senator, Johnson 3,525, Moore 1,073,
> Randall 88, Lewis 97."

The two lines are distinct AP wire flashes printed as they arrived (not a
running cumulative table — inconsistent snapshots, typical of AP flashes
compiled out of order). The basis is stated explicitly in each line ("N out
of 310 precincts") — this is unambiguously an incomplete, election-night
wire count, not a certified or even locally-compiled total.

**Numerator selection: the later/larger snapshot (31 of 310 precincts) is
the better floor candidate** (higher precinct count, later in the print
run). Contest = President, all 4 printed columns:

| Ticket | Votes |
|---|---|
| Republican (Hoover) | 3,533 |
| Democratic (Smith) | 1,799 |
| Socialist (Thomas) | 45 |
| Prohibition (Varney) | 55 |

**Contest sum = 3,533 + 1,799 + 45 + 55 = 5,432 ballots**, 31 of 310 San
Diego County precincts, per the SECOND (larger) AP wire flash.

(The earlier 17/310 flash: Republican 3,063 + Democratic 1,452 + Socialist
17 + Prohibition 19 = 4,551 — lower on every column, consistent with it
being the earlier snapshot; not used as the headline figure.)

## Verdict

**Night count FLOOR = 5,432 ballots** in the presidential contest (Hoover
3,533 + Smith 1,799 + Thomas/Socialist 45 + Varney/Prohibition 55), 31 of
310 San Diego County precincts, per the Sacramento Union's AP wire "vote by
counties" table, 1928-11-07 morning edition (later of two same-page
snapshots). `ap-remote-basis`: this is a remote-paper, raw-wire-flash floor;
both the precinct fraction (31/310 ≈ 10%) and the remote/wire nature of the
source make this a much lower floor than 1920's or 1924's San-Diego-own or
Union-table figures. Share of certified contest total: 5,432 / 71,151 =
**7.6%** (denominator: SOV 1928 President contest sum, `denominators.md`).
