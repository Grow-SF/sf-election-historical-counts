# 1916-11-07 presidential general - San Diego County night count (San Diego Union, CDNC)

Agent dossier, written as-you-go. Repo data untouched; screenshots under
`mirror/cdnc/screenshots/` (gitignored).

## Verdict

**Night count (contest floor): 24,811 ballots counted in the PRESIDENT contest**
(Hughes 12,629 + Wilson 12,182), across **152 of the 221 precincts** in San
Diego city and county, "at [2/3] o'clock this morning", printed in the
day-after San Diego Union of Wednesday 1916-11-08, page 2. No precinct table
was printed the morning after (unlike 1912); the count survives as prose in
one short article.

- The two printed candidate figures are internally consistent with the
  headline: 12,629 - 12,182 = 447 ~= "carried this county by about 450".
- Benson (Soc) and Hanly (Proh) are NOT printed, so 24,811 is a floor on the
  contest in the reported precincts (the two missing slates were small).
- Gate: masthead reads "THE SAN DIEGO UNION: WEDNESDAY MORNING, NOVEMBER 8,
  1916" == expected E+1 issue. PASS (sd_19161108_p2.png).
- Gate: contest sum <= certified county total for President: **PASS**.
  Certified SD County contest sum (SoV highest-elector, `denominators.md`) =
  36,552 (Hughes 16,978 + Wilson 16,815 + Benson 1,627 + Hanly 1,132).
  24,811 <= 36,552; per candidate: 12,629 <= 16,978 and 12,182 <= 16,815.
  (Sanity: the paper's own day+2 near-complete unofficial count is 16,864 +
  16,695 = 33,559 for the same two candidates, comfortably above 24,811.)
- Gate: precincts-reported ratio 152/221 = 0.69 <= 1. PASS.

Context: 1916 was the closest presidential race California ever had, and the
STATE was undecided (p1: "President Leads by 724 Votes With Less Than Half of
Precincts Heard From", SDDU19161108.2.12) - but San Diego's own county count
was substantial overnight: 69% of precincts summed by press time, a sharp
contrast with San Francisco's near-empty morning-after count the same day.

## Arithmetic

Night floor = 12,629 (Hughes) + 12,182 (Wilson) = **24,811** counted ballots
expressing a presidential vote, in 152 of 221 precincts (68.8%), as of the
[2/3] a.m. compilation printed E+1 morning.

Day+2 (narrative only): unofficial complete two-candidate total 33,559
(Hughes 16,864 + Wilson 16,695), all but 6 precincts (registration 160)
reported. The overnight 24,811 is 73.9% of that day+2 two-candidate total.

## Share

night share = 24,811 / 36,552 (certified President contest sum, SoV via
denominators.md) = **67.9%** (floor). Two-candidate floor: the overnight prose
omits Benson (Soc) and Hanly (Proh), so the true night count of presidential
ballots in those 152 precincts was slightly higher than 24,811.
