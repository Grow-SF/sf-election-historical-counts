## Category scoping check

Both usable figures (159,811-160,000 tallied; ~40,000 remaining) are
explicitly scoped to **absentee/mail-in ballots only** ("mail-ins had been
tabulated", "40,000 ballots dropped off at polling places" being processed by
the absentee unit). Neither article, in the excerpted text, states anything
about the status of precinct (in-person, Election-Day) ballots — no sentence
of the form "all precincts reporting" or equivalent appears in either piece.

## Arithmetic (if the 40,000-remaining figure were treated as a total)

Certified total ballots cast (SoS SOV 1988, `denominators.md` "Certified
county BALLOTS CAST" section): **890,783**.

890,783 − 40,000 = **850,783** → 850,783 / 890,783 = **95.5%** of certified.

## Soundness argument considered and rejected

Pre-permanent-VBM California practice generally had precinct ballots counted
and reported election night, with only late-arriving absentees lagging — the
kind of pattern that CAN justify a floor above 90% elsewhere in this batch
(see 1992). But that argument requires a quote establishing precinct
completion, even loosely. Here, both articles restrict themselves to the
absentee-processing operation; the registrar's own "40,000 remaining" line is
introduced as the un-finished piece of the ABSENTEE tally specifically ("the
tally was not expected to be completed"), not as a statement that "everything
else is done, only this remains." Absent that anchor, and with the computed
share sitting above the 95% suspect line, this is exactly the case the
decision rules single out: "'N late absentees remain' alone does NOT bound
mail/other categories: compute NO floor from it."

## Verdict

**NULL.** No sound election-night floor can be computed for 1988-11-08 from
the SDUB quotes. The absentee-remaining figure (~40,000, i.e., ~850,783
implied count / 95.5%) is documented here as **context only**, not a floor:
it is a single-category remainder in an era where the article does not
establish the status of the other ballot categories, and the implied share
exceeds the plausibility ceiling for treating it as airtight.
