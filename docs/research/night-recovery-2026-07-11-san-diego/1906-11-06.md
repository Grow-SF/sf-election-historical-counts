## Precincts-reported basis

- Explicit: "forty-one precincts" ("41 precincts") out of the county, stated directly
  in the article for the governor line (and repeated per-contest with slightly
  different precinct counts for other offices — e.g. 33 for Congress and Appellate
  Court, 37 for County Clerk and Recorder, 36 for Assessor — reflecting uneven
  reporting by contest at the same press deadline).
- Contest sum (2,016) is well below the certified county total for the same contest
  (7,742 sum of the five matching-ticket certified figures in `denominators.md`,
  George Gillett 3,621 + Bell 2,469 + Lewis 974 + Blanchard 174 + Langdon 504),
  consistent with a 41-precinct partial count — ratio 26.0%.

## Verdict

**Night count FLOOR = 2,016 ballots** in the governor contest (Gillett 987 + Bell 691
+ Lewis 216 + Blanchard 37 + Langdon 85), a 41-precinct partial county count as
compiled by the San Diego Union's county desk by 4:30 a.m. for its Nov. 7, 1906
morning edition. Share of certified final (7,742): **2,016 / 7,742 = 26.0%**.
