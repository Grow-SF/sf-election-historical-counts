# 1894-11-06 gubernatorial general — San Diego County night count (San Diego Union, 1894-11-07)

Agent dossier, written as-you-go. Repo untouched (no commits).

- Election: Tuesday 1894-11-06, gubernatorial general (James H. Budd-D, Morris
  M. Estee-R, Henry French-Prohibition, J. V. Webster-People's Party/Populist).
- Day-after issue: San Diego Union and Daily Bee, Wednesday morning 1894-11-07,
  issue OID `SDDU18941107` (50 sections). Issue URL:
  https://cdnc.ucr.edu/?a=d&d=SDDU18941107
- Masthead gate: PASS — "THE SAN DIEGO UNION, WEDNESDAY MORNING, NOVEMBER 7,
  1894" (visible in `mirror/cdnc/screenshots/sd_18941107_p5.png`; Nov 7 1894
  was a Wednesday).

## Precincts-reported basis

9 precincts (6 city ward-precincts + 3 county precincts) image-verified on
page 5, out of at least 11 more precincts printed in the same article whose
source image could not be located (see exclusions table) plus city wards/
precincts (Second, Third, Fifth, Sixth, Seventh-2nd, Eighth-2nd) that print no
Governor row at all (only down-ballot offices or "ahead"/margin language). The
article states the city alone had "sixteen precincts" reporting a vote of
3,482 that morning (up from 3,123 in 1892), of which only 3 precincts anywhere
in the city had "complete" counts by press time — consistent with this
dossier's thin, partial-count floor.

## Verdict

**Night count FLOOR = 756 ballots** in the Governor contest (Estee 370 + Budd
221 + French 23 + Webster 142), from 6 San Diego City ward-precincts + 3
county precincts (Chollas, Coronado, Paradise) that print a countable Governor
vote line on page 5 of the Union's Wednesday-morning (day-after) edition, and
that could be directly verified against the page image. A substantially larger
set of precincts (Escondido City, El Cajon, Oceanside, National City, La Mesa,
Otay, and others) appear in the article's own OCR text via a "Continued on
page 7" jump that could not be located on the digitized page 7 — these are
excluded from the floor rather than trusted from OCR alone, per the project's
image-verification discipline. Share of certified contest total: 756 / 7,124 =
**10.6%** (denominator from `denominators.md`, CA Blue Book 1895 p.254).

## ADDENDUM 2026-07-12: continuation located on PAGE 2 — floor revised 756 → 1,761

to read **"Continued on page 2."** (the ambiguous final glyph was a 2, not a
7; see `sd_18941107_p5_col4_escondido.png` bottom line). Page 7 and page 8
were both checked in full and carry no election content (page 7: features +
"NOTICE TO TAXPAYERS"; page 8: weather/personal/classifieds). Page 2
(`SDDU18941107.1.2`, full capture `mirror/cdnc/screenshots/sd_18941107_p2.png`)
carries the article's continuation under the standing head **"CITY AND
COUNTY. (Continued from page 5.)"** across five columns, with the remaining
county precincts. All previously-excluded precincts are now IMAGE-VERIFIED.

- **Del Mar [?]**: Budd's and Webster's trailing digits are blurred at 4x
  (`sd_18941107_p2_delmar_line4x.png`); Budd reads 20 (possibly 29), Webster
  26 (possibly 23). The reading adopted (20 + 18 + 1 + 26 = 65) is the only
  natural one that matches the precinct's printed "Total vote cast, 65"

  La Mesa, Otay, and Paradise. Row sum is 65 under any exact-total reading.

  not the candidate figures: every other contest in the same precinct block

  Associate Justices ~170), so the printed total is missing a leading "1"
  (169) or is simply wrong. The candidate figures are printed plainly
  (`sd_18941107_p2_col3_a.png`) and are INCLUDED, flagged for human review.

  guess ("Budd 18...86 votes") was actually Budd 15/total 85; its "unnamed"

  total 84) was Budd 2[0?], total 65.
