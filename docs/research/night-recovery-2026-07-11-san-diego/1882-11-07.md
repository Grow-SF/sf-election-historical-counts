## Numerator: single aggregate paragraph (target contest, GOVERNOR)

The article's heading and lead sentence, vision-read from
`sd_18821108_p3_crop_state_ticket_top.png`:

> "ELECTION RETURNS. Returns from Twenty Precincts. We have returns from
> the five [wards of] San Diego City, Bernardo, Chollas, Central, Campo,
> Forster, Fall Brook, Mt. Fairview, Mission, National, Poway, Pala, San

Precinct count check: 5 San Diego City wards + 15 named outlying precincts
(Bernardo, Chollas, Central, Campo, Forster, Fall Brook, Mt. Fairview,
Mission, National, Poway, Pala, San Luis Rey, San Pasqual, Temecula, Viejas)

1879 dossier, this article prints only the county-wide aggregate — no
precinct-by-precinct table — so there is no lower-level breakdown to
cross-check against.

Governor line ("STATE TICKET", first office listed), vision-read from
`sd_18821108_p3_crop_gov_tight3.png` (fully legible, no ambiguous digits):

from a precinct table (none was printed).

## Verdict

**Night count FLOOR = 1,103 ballots** in the Governor contest (Estee 614 +
Stoneman 489), aggregated across the named 20 San Diego County precincts (5
San Diego City wards + Bernardo, Chollas, Central, Campo, Forster, Fall
Brook, Mt. Fairview, Mission, National, Poway, Pala, San Luis Rey, San
Pasqual, Temecula, Viejas), printed in the San Diego Union's Wednesday
1882-11-08 morning edition under "ELECTION RETURNS. Returns from Twenty
Precincts." As with the 1879 dossier, the paper explicitly states it is
giving only the aggregate result because the lateness of the hour at which
returns arrived precluded printing precinct-by-precinct details, so while
1,103 is the only county-wide Governor figure printed in this issue, it may
not represent every precinct in the county (no total precinct count is
given to confirm full coverage). Share of certified final: **NULL — no
certified denominator found** (`denominators.md` 1882-11-07 entry: exhausted
the SOV, CA Blue Book/Governmental Roster, and Tribune Almanac; none is
digitized for this year). The only available comparison is indicative, not
citable as a share of the certified final: this dossier's night floor 1,103
against the Los Angeles Herald's own explicitly-incomplete ~1,466 dispatch
total (Estee 747 + Stoneman 719, ~10 precincts still outstanding) gives
1,103 / 1,466 ≈ 75%. This gate stays open pending discovery of a certified
source.
