## Numerator: single aggregate paragraph (target contest, GOVERNOR)

The article's lead sentence, vision-read verbatim from
`sd_18790904_p1_crop_election_gov2.png`/`_gov3.png`:

> "We have the returns from the five Wards of San Diego, and the precincts
> of National, San Luis Rey and Forster; and we give the aggregate result,
> the lateness of the hour at which returns are received precluding any
> details. The vote stands:"

Unlike the 1871, 1875, and 1884 SD Union election articles (which print a
precinct-by-precinct table), this 1879 article gives ONLY the county-wide
aggregate — no per-ward or per-precinct breakdown is printed anywhere in
this issue, so there is no lower-level table to sum or cross-check against.
This is itself a data point: the paper explicitly attributes the absence of
detail to how late the returns came in.

Governor line, vision-read from `sd_18790904_p1_crop_election_gov4.png`
(fully legible, no ambiguous digits):

> "For Governor—Perkins, 331; Glenn, 230; White, 53."

**Contest sum = 331 (Perkins) + 230 (Glenn) + 53 (White) = 614 ballots**
counted in the Governor contest, aggregated across 8 precincts (San Diego
City's five Wards, plus National, San Luis Rey, and Forster precincts) as
printed in the Union's Thursday 1879-09-04 morning edition. This is a
county-wide aggregate as printed, not a sum independently reconstructed
from a precinct table (none was printed).

## Verdict

**Night count FLOOR = 614 ballots** in the Governor contest (Perkins 331 +
Glenn 230 + White 53), aggregated across San Diego City's five Wards plus
National, San Luis Rey, and Forster precincts, printed in the San Diego
Union's Thursday 1879-09-04 morning edition under "THE ELECTION." The paper
explicitly states it is giving only the aggregate result because the
lateness of the hour at which returns arrived precluded printing precinct
details — so while 614 is the only county-wide Governor figure printed in
this issue, it is presented by the paper itself as an incomplete/rushed
count, and likely does not include every county precinct (no total precinct
count is given to confirm coverage). Share of certified final: **43.5%**
(614 / 1,412 certified; certified figure from `denominators.md` 1879-09-03
entry, Tribune Almanac for 1880 p.48).
