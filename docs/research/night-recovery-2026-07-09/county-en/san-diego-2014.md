All three articles cite the identical ~180,000-remaining figure and
explicitly state the countywide count had NOT been updated since election
night (no new results Wednesday; next update 5pm Thursday) - this is the
"held" non-circular leg proving 509,214 counted / 180,000 remaining is the
frozen election-night state, not a later canvass number.

**Arithmetic:** 509,214 (directly stated, sourced to CA SoS live count) /
692,434 certified final = 73.54%. Cross-check: 509,214 + 180,000 = 689,214,
within 0.47% of the certified final 692,434 (consistent with "about"
rounding on the 180,000 figure). Comparable: true (genuine election-night
value, held through Thursday per the "no new results Wednesday" statement).
Distinguish from the race-specific "46,000 ballots still to be counted"
figure quoted in the DeMaio-Peters piece - that is the 52nd Congressional
District's own outstanding count, NOT the countywide total, and is not used
here.
