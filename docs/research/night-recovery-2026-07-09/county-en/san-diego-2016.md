to 856,937 (63.6% of the 2016 total, up from 56% in 2012), so with full
precinct completion (489,576) but a much larger outstanding VBM pool, a
lower overall election-night share than 2012 (60.52%) is the expected
confound (heavier late/growing vote-by-mail volume depressing the
election-night share independent of any counting-tech change), not a sign
this is a first-tranche number (53.96% is nowhere near half of SF's 66%
like-for-like 2016 share, which would flag ~33%). Comparable: true.
