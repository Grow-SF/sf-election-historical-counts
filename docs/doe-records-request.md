# Public Records Request — SF Department of Elections

**How to send:** San Francisco uses the NextRequest portal
(sanfrancisco.nextrequest.com) for public records; the Department of
Elections can also be reached at sfvote@sfgov.org. Confirm the current
channel before sending. Address to the Department's Custodian of Records.

**Suggested subject:** Public Records Request — election-night and daily
canvass results releases (historical)

---

To the Custodian of Records, San Francisco Department of Elections:

Under the California Public Records Act (Gov. Code § 7920 et seq.) and the
San Francisco Sunshine Ordinance (Admin. Code Ch. 67), I request copies of
the following records. I am the research lead at GrowSF, building a public,
fully-sourced dataset of how quickly San Francisco has reported its vote
over time. The Department's own results releases are the authoritative
source for several elections where no other record survives, and I would be
grateful for your help.

**What I'm looking for, in plain terms:** for each election listed below,
the results the Department released *on election night* and on the *days
that followed during the canvass* — that is, the dated "Summary Report" /
"Statement of the Vote" files (and, for ranked-choice contests, the
preliminary round-by-round reports) as they were issued before
certification. Each release typically shows ballots counted, precincts
reported, and turnout as of a stated date and time. I am specifically
interested in those interim, time-stamped snapshots, not only the final
certified totals.

**Primary request — elections 1982 through 2014** (these are the records
most likely retained in the Department's results archive; for 2008 and
2009 these "Summary Report" PDFs were briefly posted to the Department's
website and I have recovered a few, so I know the format well. For the
1982–1986 elections I already hold mid-canvass totals, so I know dated
interim releases were issued and am hopeful they survive):

For each, the single most valuable item is **the last results release issued
the night of the election** — the final snapshot posted before the Department
stopped counting for the night, reflecting every ballot (vote-by-mail and
in-person) tallied that night rather than an early partial bulletin — followed
by the **daily canvass releases** through certification. If several time-stamped
releases went out over the course of election night, the latest one is the one
I most want; earlier partials from the same night are welcome but secondary.

**Secondary request — elections 1972 through 1980** (good-faith; I
understand dated interim releases from this era may not have been retained
beyond the permanent certified Statement of the Vote): any retained
election-night or interim canvass results bulletins for the June 1972,
November 1974, June 1976, August 1977, November 1978, December 1979, June
1980, and August 1980 San Francisco elections. If only the certified
Statements of the Vote survive for these, please let me know and I will not
ask you to search further.

**Format and fees:** Electronic copies (PDF, or the original data files in
any format — XML, TSV, CSV, or scans) sent by email or shared drive are
ideal and avoid duplication costs. If responsive records exist only on
paper or microfilm, please advise on the volume and any fees before
proceeding. As this request supports a free public-interest project, I ask
that any fees be waived or minimized where the Ordinance allows.

I am happy to narrow or prioritize this request to ease the burden on your
staff — if it helps, start with the November 2010 and November 2006 night
releases and the 2011 ranked-choice reports. Please acknowledge receipt and
let me know the expected timeline. Thank you for your work and your help.
